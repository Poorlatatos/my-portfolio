Project: ASTEROIDS

Welcome to ASTEROIDS, a modern reimagining of the iconic retro game from the golden age of arcades. Developed in Unity, this project combines nostalgic gameplay with sleek, modern design. Immerse yourself in a fast-paced world of interstellar battles, dynamic physics, and endless challenges.

Experience the thrill of piloting your ship through treacherous asteroid fields!

Key Features

  • **Dynamic Asteroid Physics**: Each asteroid has realistic collision physics, breaking into smaller fragments on impact.
  • **Upgradeable Spaceship**: Unlock new abilities like rapid fire, shields, and hyperspace jumps as you progress.
  • **Adaptive Difficulty**: Levels become progressively challenging, with faster asteroids and enemy ships joining the fray.
  • **Leaderboards**: Compete globally to claim the highest score.

Customize and upgrade your spaceship to dominate the asteroid belt.

Gameplay Mechanics

At its core, ASTEROIDS stays true to its roots: the player controls a nimble spaceship in a top-down perspective, navigating an ever-changing asteroid field. The challenge lies in maneuvering through the chaos while using your ship's weapons to destroy incoming threats.

Developer's Reflection

Remaking ASTEROIDS was really a learning journey for me since I'm not a game developer by trade, and as such I'm just a rookie at it. Thus I had to learn a lot of new things, such as Unity, C#, and game design principles that span over the course of a few months. I'm proud of the result, and I hope you enjoy playing it as much as I enjoyed making it.
